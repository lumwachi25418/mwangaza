About us

Mwanganza Melodies Singers is a vibrant gospel music group born from the heart of a local church choir. It brings together mature men and women who are not only neighbors but also united in faith as members of the same congregation. Rooted in fellowship and love for Christ, this group was formed with a shared desire to glorify God through music and to minister hope, healing, and truth to the world. Their mission is simple yet powerful: to sing beautiful, spirit-filled songs and preach the gospel through their voices. Whether in worship services, community gatherings, or special events, Mwanganza Melodies Singers seek to be vessels of light—mwangaza—shining the message of Christ wherever they go. Their harmonies are more than melodies; they are testimonies of unity, maturity, and a deep commitment to serve God through song. “Let the message of Christ dwell among you richly… singing to God with gratitude in your hearts.” — Colossians 3:16 (NIV)
